This plugin from owagu.com Will grab the entire clickbank marketplace contents and pump them into your wordpress. Build webshops with your affiliate ID embedded. Have tens of thousands of products online in mere seconds.

== Description ==

Clickbank has always been a favorite amongst affiliates due to it's high payout ratio's, but it's always proven difficult to get the entire clickbank marketplace molded in to one website. Now, with help from owagu.com, you can simply push the entire clickbank market into your own wordpress installation.

 The script will create tens of thousands of posts on the fly, fully categorized, fully SEO optimized and ready to be indexed by search engines. Naturally, each and every link created will have your own clickbank ID embedded right inside.

Simple & clean moneymaking without any fuss ... !!!
